about us

Welcome To The Tawa Grill Indian & Hakka Chinese

The Tawa Grill Indian & Hakka Chinese takes pride in providing exceptional customer service, as well as high-quality and consistent food and beverages. We provide traditional Hakka Chinese(Bombay Style) dishes. Our chefs prepare original dishes with reverence and respect for the cuisine. We invite you to join along and take part in the adventure.
